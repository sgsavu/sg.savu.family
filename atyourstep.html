At Your Step AppTeam Project

UX and Concept Design

At Your Step is a mobile application that provides a matching service between people self-isolating as a result of a positive COVID test or needing to shield because of a health condition/old age, and volunteers who are willing to provide support by buying groceries and completing a contact free delivery to the self-isolating or vulnerable person.
