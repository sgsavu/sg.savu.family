ElusiveTeam Game Project

Java

Elusive is a 2D platformer racing game for up to 10 players. I have been part of the development team for this game as lead system designer where I've put to practice systems such as Tracking Minimap, Inventory System, Map Random Generation, Parsing and Interpretation, Powerups, Assets and Animation Handling and also contributed in majority of the art style and level design sections of the game.

 

 

During this project I was able to fully extend my Java skillset and dive deep into numerous Software Design & Engineering techniques which I am sure will offer me plenty of experience stepping into the world of IT.
