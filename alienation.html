Alienation Twin-Stick Shooter

Mini Game in Java

Alienation is a twin-stick shooter top down game in which you have to defeat countless waves of invading aliens. The scope of the game is to survive and successfully eliminate hordes of enemies that are trying to kill you.

 

 

Saving the world is your main objective, thus, travelling to their home world represents no challenge for you. On death, aliens drop power ups such as damage boost, speed boots, time freezing ancient stones, and many other that will help you in your journey across the galaxy. It features multiple fantastic maps with a variety of environments and themes that will immerse you into the game's atmosphere. The difficulty increases each level as you approach the final boss.

The game is completely made in Java using the JSFML library. Game copy available on demand or through the button below.
