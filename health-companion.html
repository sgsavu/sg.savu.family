Diabetes Health Companion

Mobile Android Application

Developed a mobile android health application designed to act as a companion for patients diagnosed with diabetes. After the initial release the scope of the app was broadened to a more general health app that will benefit all individuals suffering of any condition.

 

 

Features of the app include: storing information about your medicine, setting custom reminders for the user's medicine, a meal planner using the Spoonacular API tailored around the user's needs and an exercise manager that will allow the user to stay healthy.

All of the features mentioned above were implemented through the use of a Google Firebase database that communicates with the app at all times.
