Flatty Flatmate Finder

Android App UI Design

Designed the User Interface for a mobile Android application that connects people together and helps with the struggle of securing a flat. The app offers the possibility to either list your flat for people to see or for you to search other flats.

 

 

The profile feature allows for individuals with common interests to connect and share a flat reducing the often mentioned problem of "bad flatmates". The app should allow people to either contact each other by phone or through the live text messaging system available within the app.

Numerous ease of use features such as the favorites tab, settings for range, gender or even the ability to switch between a 2D or 3D view of the map, make it a tough competitor in the market.
