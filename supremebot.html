Supreme Bot Fast Checkout Bot

Script in Python and Selenium

Supreme is a very limited brand releasing high demand clothing. So limited that each capsule sells out in less than 1-5 mins. After a product is sold out it is gone forever, thus the value of that product rises tremendously as there are many other people trying to purchase it from a 3rd party.

 

 

Automating the process of checking out a product on drop day increases the chances that you will cop the piece that you desire. What would usually be a 30-60 seconds checkout time turns into a 5-7 seconds with the touch of a button.

My script allows the user to open a remote controlled version of Google Chrome/Mozilla Firefox that will traverse the pages and fill in the shipping information required by Supreme ultimately securing the product. Beating the competition is key to profit.
